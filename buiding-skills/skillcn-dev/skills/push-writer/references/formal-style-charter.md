# Formal Style Charter

All formal writer outputs must obey these rules derived from `Architecture.md`.

## Core writing posture

- write as a real researcher, not as a chat assistant
- use accurate, objective, written, and elegant prose
- avoid oral filler, broken short fragments, and casual phrasing
- do not exaggerate success or failure

## Independence constraints

- the document must stand on its own for an outside reader
- remove project “presence” and internal workflow traces
- do not leak private file names, paths, or internal record systems
- do not write as if arguing with an invisible opponent
- do not preserve chat-tone defense or explanation posture

## Honesty constraints

- do not use polished wording to hide unstable facts
- do not convert speculation into fact
- keep uncertainty, limitations, and evidence boundaries explicit

## Terminology constraints

- keep terminology stable
- avoid unnecessary English when a proper Chinese expression exists
- when an English technical term must appear, introduce it carefully and consistently
