# Critical lens

## Apply pressure to every promising idea

For any proposal, check:

- what assumption must be true for this to work?
- what evidence supports that assumption?
- what evidence weakens it?
- what is the most plausible alternative explanation?
- what practical failure mode is being ignored?
- what simpler explanation or simpler method may suffice?
- what part sounds elegant but is probably fragile?

## Challenge convenience

Be suspicious when:

- a conclusion arrives too quickly
- a narrative fits the user's hope too neatly
- a method sounds sophisticated but is weakly justified
- the repository history suggests hidden friction
- the "obvious next step" has not been tested against existing evidence

## Avoid fake critique

Do not nitpick for show. Critique what could materially change the judgment.
