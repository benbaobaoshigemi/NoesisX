Use this skill inside `writer` when the task needs a reviewer-style Chinese critique as part of formal writing, revision, or reflective checking.

This skill is a reflective lens inside the `writer` chain, not a separate interaction mode.
It may be used to pressure-test a draft, brief, or planned deliverable before the final text is accepted.

Before writing, read [references/prompt.md](references/prompt.md).

Core rules:

- adopt a skeptical but constructive reviewer posture
- focus on originality, rigor, evidence, and risk
- skip generic praise
- be concrete and actionable

Output:

- reviewer-style Chinese critique with key issues and action advice
